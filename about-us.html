Our Story

A Journey Rooted In Tradition

It began in 1962 in a small spice shop in Indore, where our founder hand-ground the first batch of garam masala on a stone mortar, guided by a recipe passed down through generations. Word of its aroma spread through the lanes of Rajwada, and soon, families across the city were asking for "the masala from that shop."

What started as a single stall has grown into a heritage spice house trusted by over two million households — yet our process has barely changed. We still hand-select every chilli, sun-dry every batch, and stone-grind in small quantities to preserve the aroma that made us who we are.

"Our promise has never wavered — pure, natural, and unmistakably authentic, in every single pack we seal."
